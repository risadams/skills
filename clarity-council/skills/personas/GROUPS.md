# Persona Groups

Each group below is a small, purpose-built panel for focused council sessions.

## Product Delivery Core

Use for prioritization, scope, and delivery tradeoffs.

- product-owner
- product-manager
- scrum-master
- tech-lead
- qa-engineer

## Platform and Reliability

Use for architecture, operations, risk, and production stability.

- senior-architect
- ops-architect
- devops-engineer
- site-reliability-engineer
- security-expert

## Growth and Content

Use for audience, messaging, distribution, and conversion work.

- growth-strategist
- social-media-marketer
- content-creator
- customer-advocate
- technical-writer

## Research and Knowledge

Use for evidence gathering, analysis, and durable knowledge capture.

- researcher
- statistics-expert
- knowledge-manager
- data-engineer
- technical-writer

## Governance and Resourcing

Use for funding, policy, risk, and organizational constraints.

- business-owner
- financial-officer
- risk-manager
- compliance-officer
- finops-engineer

## Personal Productivity

Use for daily briefings, focus-block proposals, calendar/task triage, and any "what do I do next" framing.

- personal-assistant
- psychologist
- scrum-master

## Communication and Tone

Use for decoding messages, drafting/softening responses, and reviewing tone of comments before they post.

- psychologist
- customer-advocate
- technical-writer
- content-creator

## Investigation and Diagnosis

Use for bug triage, root-cause hypothesis, and feature analysis where evidence-gathering rigor matters more than speed.

- researcher
- senior-architect
- qa-engineer
- devils-advocate

## Stakeholder Reporting

Use for sprint reviews, status updates, and any artifact destined for leadership or external audiences. Pairs visual rigor with durable-knowledge capture.

- statistics-expert
- infographics-expert
- product-owner
- knowledge-manager
- scrum-master

## Accessibility and Inclusivity

Use for slides, dashboards, public docs, and anywhere readability/contrast/alt-text matter.

- accessibility-expert
- ux-designer
- graphic-designer
- technical-writer
