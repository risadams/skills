# Persona Groups

Each group below is a small, purpose-built panel for focused council sessions.

## Product Delivery Core

Use for prioritization, scope, and delivery tradeoffs.

- product-owner
- product-manager
- scrum-master
- tech-lead
- qa-engineer

## Platform and Reliability

Use for architecture, operations, risk, and production stability.

- senior-architect
- ops-architect
- devops-engineer
- site-reliability-engineer
- security-expert

## Growth and Content

Use for audience, messaging, distribution, and conversion work.

- growth-strategist
- social-media-marketer
- content-creator
- customer-advocate
- technical-writer

## Research and Knowledge

Use for evidence gathering, analysis, and durable knowledge capture.

- researcher
- statistics-expert
- knowledge-manager
- data-engineer
- technical-writer

## Governance and Resourcing

Use for funding, policy, risk, and organizational constraints.

- business-owner
- financial-officer
- risk-manager
- compliance-officer
- finops-engineer
